TradeDocs

TradeDocs.org StandardDocument (abbreviated as StanDoc) is a family of document models that forms the basis of Freight Trust’s TradeDoc, which is used to represent our legal parity digital smart contracts. These documents are based off of ISO Standards and similarly structured standards. It itself is an instantiation of BasicDocument (BasicDoc)

These are the principle document structures that form the basis for our primitives.

TradeDocs itself is a document model can form the basis of multiple standard document types, including:

  • Bills of Lading

  • Warehouse Receipts

  • Letters of Credit

  • Forward Contracts

  • Advanced Manifests

  • Bill of Materials

  • and more…​

The Relaton Bibliography UML models used within these models are located here: Relaton BibModels
